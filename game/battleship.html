Got It!

Battleship is a guessing game for two players (or Player vs PC). The locations of the fleet are concealed from you. In this mode you have to call the shots on the grid to destroy all of the opponents ships.


There are 3 different size ships on the grid!

After you destroy all the ships, the message will pop up stating you accuracy and number of shots you took. You can use those statistics to practice and develop better strategies and tactics for the next game!
